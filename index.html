Take a vacation!

This may be about the most purposeful and relaxing vacation you will ever take! This personal development nature getaway will help you transform your life while getting away from your everyday life in a renewing way.

Experience a new you...

Through nature: We are set in a natural setting where you can go hiking, explore, or simply meditate in nature.

Through new skills: We have workshops to allow you to learn several new hobbies including painting, meditation, aerobics, and more!

Through connection: For those who want, we have a common area and nightly campfire event which are designed to allow connection with other guests.

Through guided growth: For those who want to dive deep into their personal development, we have programs specifically designed to the level of personal growth you are looking for. Whatever time-frame you're looking at, there's a program for you!
